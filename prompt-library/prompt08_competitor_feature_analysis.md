# Prompt 08 — Competitor Feature Analysis

## Prompt Text

You are a product strategy analyst evaluating competitor products in the [industry] sector.

Our product context:
[Insert description of our product, target users, and key capabilities]

Using the competitor product information provided below, analyse their features and identify:

1. Key features offered by competitors  
2. Strengths of the competitor product  
3. Potential weaknesses or gaps  
4. Opportunities where our product could differentiate from competitors 
5. Strategic insights that could influence product development

Present the analysis in a structured format that product managers can use during product strategy discussions.

Competitor product information:
[Insert competitor product details here]

---

## Intended Workflow / Task

This prompt supports product teams in analysing competitor products to understand market positioning and identify opportunities for differentiation.

---

## Problem Being Solved

Product teams often need to review competitor products manually, which can take significant time and may lead to incomplete comparisons. This prompt allows the analyst to navigate through the important parts of the analysis and use the insights for development.

---

## Automation Potential

This prompt enables faster competitor feature analysis by summarising strengths, weaknesses, and strategic opportunities from available information.

---

## Risks and Limitations

AI-generated competitor analysis is dependent on the completeness and accuracy of the provided information. Potential limitations include:

- Incomplete competitive landscape, if important competitors or features are not included in the input data.

- Outdated or biased product information, which may lead to inaccurate comparisons.

- Oversimplified strategic insights, since AI cannot fully assess market dynamics, user behaviour, or organisational strategy.

The analysis should therefore be treated as a preliminary overview rather than a comprehensive market assessment. Product teams should validate the insights using reliable market research before making strategic product decisions.
