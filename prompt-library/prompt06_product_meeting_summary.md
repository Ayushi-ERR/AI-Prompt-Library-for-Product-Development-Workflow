# Prompt 06 — Product Meeting Summary

## Prompt Text

You are a product operations assistant responsible for documenting product team meetings.

Using the meeting notes provided below, generate a structured meeting summary that includes:

1. Key discussion topics  
2. Important decisions made  
3. Action items  
4. Responsible team members (if mentioned)  
5. Deadlines or next steps  

Present the output in a clear format that can be shared with the product and development teams.

Meeting notes:
[Insert meeting notes here]

---

## Intended Workflow / Task

This prompt is used after product team meetings to convert raw meeting notes into structured summaries and actionable items.

---

## Problem Being Solved

Product teams frequently take informal notes during meetings, which can make it difficult to track decisions, responsibilities, and follow-up actions.

---

## Automation Potential

This prompt automates the process of converting meeting notes into structured summaries and action lists, helping teams maintain clear documentation and accountability.

---

## Risks and Limitations

AI-generated summaries may miss context or incorrectly interpret discussions. The meeting facilitator should review the summary before sharing it with the team.
