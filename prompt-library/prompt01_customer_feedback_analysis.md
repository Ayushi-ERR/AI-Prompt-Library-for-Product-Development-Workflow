# Prompt 01 — Customer Feedback Analysis

## Prompt Text

You are a product analyst at a comapny in [industry] sector, supporting a product development team.

Analyse the customer feedback provided below and identify the following:

1. Key pain points of the customers
2. Feature requests by customers
3. Overall customer sentiment : negative, postive or netural
4. Recurring themes  
5. Recommended priority areas for product improvement  

Present the output using clear headings and concise business language.

Customer feedback:
[Insert Feedback here]

---

## Intended Workflow / Task

This prompt is designed to support product teams in analysing customer feedback collected from surveys, customer support tickets, and product reviews. It will also help team to navigate through the sentiment analysis and focus on the ones that need improvements.

---

## Problem Being Solved

Product development teams often receive large amounts of unstructured customer feedback. Manually analysing and summarising this feedback to find insights can be time-consuming and inconsistent. Product team can use this prompt to segregate the key points in the customer feedback instead of going through it manually.

---

## Automation Potential

This prompt enables automated analysis of customer feedback by extracting key insights and sentiment. It helps product teams identify common issues and improvement opportunities without manually reviewing each response and it will help them to divide works on the basis of sentiments.

---

## Risks and Limitations

AI-generated feedback analysis relies on the clarity and completeness of the customer comments provided. Potential limitations include:

- Misinterpretation of sentiment or tone, particularly when feedback includes sarcasm, informal language, or ambiguous phrasing.

- Overgeneralisation of customer concerns, where AI may group unrelated feedback into the same theme.

- Incomplete context, since the system does not have access to broader customer behaviour data or historical feedback trends.

The generated analysis should therefore be treated as an initial insight summary. Product teams should review the findings and validate them against additional customer data before making product decisions.
