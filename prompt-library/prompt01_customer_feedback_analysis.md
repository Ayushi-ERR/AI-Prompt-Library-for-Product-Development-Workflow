# Prompt 01 — Customer Feedback Analysis

## Prompt Text

You are a product analyst supporting a product development team.

Analyse the customer feedback provided below and identify the following:

1. Key pain points of the customers
2. Feature requests by customers
3. Overall customer sentiment : negative, postive or netural
4. Recurring themes  
5. Recommended priority areas for product improvement  

Present the output using clear headings and concise business language.

Customer feedback:
[ ----Insert Feedback here----]

---

## Intended Workflow / Task

This prompt is designed to support product teams in analysing customer feedback collected from surveys, customer support tickets, and product reviews. It will also help team to navigate through the sentiment analysis and focus on the ones that need improvements.

---

## Problem Being Solved

Product development teams often receive large amounts of unstructured customer feedback. Manually analysing and summarising this feedback to find insights can be time-consuming and inconsistent.

---

## Automation Potential

This prompt enables automated analysis of customer feedback by extracting key insights and sentiment. It helps product teams identify common issues and improvement opportunities without manually reviewing each response and it will help them to divide works on the basis of sentiments.

---

## Risks and Limitations

AI may misinterpret context, sarcasm, or customer sentiment. Human review is still necessary before making any strategic product decisions based on the analysis by this prompt.
