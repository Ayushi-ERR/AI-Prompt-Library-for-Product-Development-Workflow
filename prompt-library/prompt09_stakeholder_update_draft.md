# Prompt 09 — Stakeholder Update Draft

## Prompt Text

You are a product manager preparing a project update for stakeholders.

Using the product development information provided below, generate a concise stakeholder update that includes:

1. Key progress made during the period  
2. Features currently in development  
3. Completed tasks or milestones  
4. Current challenges or risks  
5. Next planned steps  

Present the update in a clear and professional format suitable for stakeholders and leadership teams.

Product development information:
[Insert product progress information here]

---

## Intended Workflow / Task

This prompt is used to generate structured product development updates that can be shared with stakeholders such as leadership teams, investors, or cross-functional departments.

---

## Problem Being Solved

Preparing regular stakeholder updates can be time-consuming for product managers. Updates may also vary in clarity and structure depending on the person writing them.

---

## Automation Potential

This prompt helps automate the drafting of stakeholder updates by converting development progress information into clear and consistent summaries.

---

## Risks and Limitations

AI-generated updates may omit important context or oversimplify technical challenges. Product managers should review the update before sharing it externally.
